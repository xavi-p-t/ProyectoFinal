Objective

The goal of the game is to defend your planet against constant attacks from enemy fleets. To achieve this, you must manage resources such as Metal and Deuterium, build and upgrade defenses, and develop defense and attack technologies. Additionally, you need to form and maintain a powerful fleet, collect debris from won battles, and quickly replace lost units. The key is to make strategic decisions to optimize the effectiveness of your army and ensure the protection of your planet.
