Objective

The goal of the game is to withstand enemy attacks for as long as possible. Throughout the game, you must upgrade the available technologies to their maximum level and build a variety of troops and defenses. These upgrades and constructions will allow you to face enemy attacks more effectively and increase your chances of victory. As you progress, the key will be to balance your investment in technology and the construction of defensive units to facilitate battles and ensure the survival of your planet.

Main Menu

Here is the main section, where you can choose the option you need. You can view the planet's statistics, upgrade its technology, build defenses and spaceships for attacks, and see a report on the battles that have taken place since you started the game.

View Stats

View Planet Stats: In this section, the statistics of our planet are displayed. The statistics usually include:
  • Technology: Here, the level of attack and defense technology will be shown.
  • Defense: Here, the number of defenses you have will be displayed, such as Missile Launcher, Ion Cannon, and Plasma Cannon. The more you have, the stronger your defenses will be.
  • Fleet: Here, the fleet you possess will be shown, including the Light Hunter, Heavy Hunter, Battle Ship, and Armored Ship.
  • Resources: There are two types of resources, metal and deuterium.

Build Options

This section allows you to build various troops and defenses.
Once you choose what you want to construct, whether it's a defense or an attack unit, a menu will appear where you can select what you want to build. For example, all the available troops you can construct are shown here.
After selecting the troop or defense, you will be asked to specify the quantity.

Upgrade Technology

This section is for upgrading technology. Upgrading technology can be very useful, as it allows you to increase the damage and defense of your defenses and weapons.
You will have two sections: one for upgrading defense technology and another for upgrading attack technology. Each time you level up, the next level will cost more deuterium.

Battle Reports

In the "View Battle Reports" section, you can review the battles that have occurred. You need to select the battle whose data you want to display. The higher the number, the more recent the battle.
Once the battle is selected, the statistics of the planet's and enemy's weapons and defenses, along with the cost of damages and waste generated, will be displayed.
If you choose to view more details of the battle, information such as damage inflicted, remaining defense, and whether the troop was eliminated will appear. Additionally, it will show when the attacker changes and many more details.

New thread is comming

Once the message "New Thread is Coming" appears, the menu will change, and a new option called "View Thread Comming" will appear, displaying the thread's data.
The data shown in the new thread includes the number of ships that are going to attack you.

Tips

Tip 1

You can use a very large number when creating troops or defenses to build as many as you can.

Tip 2

Periodically, check the planet's statistics to monitor the amount of defenses, fleet, and resources you have.

Tips 3

Upgrade your technology as soon as possible to defend and attack more effectively.

frequently asked question

FAQ

Why does an attack message appear every 3 minutes?

▼

The game is designed so that the enemy attacks the planet every 3 minutes.

What should you create first, defenses or fleet?

▼

You should create both in a balanced way to have strong defense and offense.

What technology should we focus on improving first?

▼

We should strive for balanced improvement in both our defense and offense to achieve an optimal level.
